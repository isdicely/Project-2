World Happiness Overview







Introduction

The world we once knew in 2019 has become only a memory and during this past year the world has experienced grief and uncertainty that has changed us all. The World Happiness Data provides us with a glimpse of the world’s resilience during one of the most difficult times in this century. Which countries have been able to find a silver lining during this turbulent global pandemic? Our team has created several visualizations of the World Happiness Data to help answer this question and to give an understanding of some factors that contribute to a country’s happiness.




The World Happiness Data

The World Happiness Report utilizes information from the Gallup World survey. The Gallup World Survey uses telephone and face-to-face interviewing in randomly selected households all across the globe. The typical survey size is 1,000 individuals. Each index is calculated by asking participants a series of yes or no questions which then receive a numerical value. There are a total of nineteen indexes, seven of which are used to calculate a Happiness Score which we will utilize to interpret the happiness of countries in 2019 and 2020.
